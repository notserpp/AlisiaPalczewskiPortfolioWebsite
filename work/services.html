What I do?

Here are some of my expertise

Adobe Premiere

Five years ago through a high school arts program, I was taught the basics of Premiere. This is where my love of editing began and since then have expanded my knowledge of the program and used it for all of my editing projects.

Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator

My internship at Pacific Aviation and Lease Management and various student projects have expanded my knowledge of the programs. I rely on Photoshop and Illustrator for any visual arts project I am tasked with, specifically when it comes to creating graphics.

Pro Tools

I learned ProTools through a Cinematic Sound Design class at SFSU under Daniel Olmsted. My internship at Outpost Studios helped me understand more complicated features of the program and I have learned to use it under a professional studio environment.

Visual Production Management + Client Interface

My internships have provided me with an understanding of what it is like to produce and edit creative content in a team environment for the specific needs of clientele.


Scriptwriting + Directing

I am currently writing, directing, and editing my thesis film, "Made With Love". This hands on experience has provided me with the knowledge of creating a professional film project from start to finish.
